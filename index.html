Johannes Gutenberg

(1393-1406)

Johannes Gutenberg was born in Rhine, German in 12th century.

Invention

He was known for inventing the movable-type printing machine, leading to the circulation of printed books, which influences the later renaissance and religious reform.

Legacy

One of his known book would be the "Gutenberg Bible" which is believed to be the first full-scale printed book in the world.
