Johannes Gutenberg


(1390-1468)


Johannes Gutenberg was born in Mainz, Germany in 12th century. His career as an inventor started in his later year when he was exiled in Strasboug in 1450s.

Invention


He was known for inventing the movable-type printing machine, leading to the circulation of printed books, which influences the later renaissance and religious reform.

Legacy


One of his known book would be the "Gutenberg Bible" which is believed to be the first full-scale printed book in Europe.
